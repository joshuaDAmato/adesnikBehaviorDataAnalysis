`brainbox.metrics`: Functions for assesing spike unit quality
===============================================================

The purpose of the `metrics` submodule is to provide utilities for quantifying how stable a single
spiking unit, as identified by spike sorting, is over time. It also includes functions for
assesing level of noise in the unit and drift.
